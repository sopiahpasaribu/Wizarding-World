Immerse yourself in the magic of the wizarding world, where the boundaries of reality are pushed to the limit. The first Harry Potter Books, Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, was published by Bloomsbury in 1997 to immediate popular and critical acclaim. Since then, six further best-selling books, three companion books, a playscript, and two screenplays have followed. With over 500 million copies sold worldwide, the Harry Potter series has become the best-selling book series in history, translated into over 80 languages.
